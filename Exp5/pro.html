Shift Reduce Parser Simulator - Procedure

  1. Read the theory section on Shift Reduce Parsing.
  2. Take the pretest quiz.
  3. Open the Simulation page.
  4. Select an input string from the available examples.
  5. Click Start to initialize the Shift Reduce Parser.
  6. Observe the initial stack and input buffer contents.
  7. Click Next to execute parsing actions step by step.
  8. Analyze each Shift and Reduce operation performed by the parser.
  9. Observe how handles are reduced according to grammar productions.
  10. Continue until the parser reaches the Accept state.
  11. Study the final stack configuration and parsing result.
  12. Take the posttest quiz.
